Destek

ZERDAVA uses state-of-the-art deletion algorithms and international standards used by government and military organizations for secure file erasure.

Depending on version, ZERDAVA comes with erasing algorithms like HMG InfoSec No.5, DoD 5220.22 SSD and others, including: DoD 5220.22-ME, US Air Force (AFSSI-5020), US Army AR380-19, DoD 5220.22-M ECE, BSI/VS-ITR TL-03423, CSEC ITSG-06, NATO Standard, and Gutmann.
